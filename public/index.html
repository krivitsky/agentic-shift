first meetup — 78 attendees — hosted by egym.
a packed room dug into agentic software and what it does to organizations:
  • cutting agentic slop by wiring direct feedback signals for agents
  • why individual ai speed gains don't compound at org scale
  • establishing agentic workflows that stick
speakers: martin westphal, alexey krivitsky, cem freimoser.

agentic engineering AND organizational impact

we are committed to continuing both themes — engineering and organizational aspects — as we are interested in systemic, global improvements for organizations and society. and these sides of the same story reinforce each other and can't be treated separately.

so, topics like 10x org raised by alexey are to be expected at about a 30% rate compared to engineering topics. we hope this offers a real differentiator versus other meetups.
